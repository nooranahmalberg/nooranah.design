Equipping employers with insights on learning

Role

In 2018, I was a junior designer on the product team at Go1, a learning management system and content aggregator for online learning.

Problem

To defend their position in the online learning space, Go1 recognised a need to differentiate their service from other learning providers, especially since users could learn content available on Go1 directly from the providers.

Solution

Empower team managers who assign learning with insight into the learning trends of their industry so that they can remain competitive.
