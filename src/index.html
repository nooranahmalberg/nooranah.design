Nooranah Malberg

I am Nooranah Malberg (she/her), a Malay-Caucasian woman who designs interfaces remotely at a Jr-level in Brisbane (Meanjin). I grew up in a place once known as Temasek. And I really care about the future of the internet.

I prefer to recognize the practice of design as care-work rather than a means towards creative disruption. This notion is partly inspired by an excellent article on Bloomberg.com titled "What It Means to Design a Space for ‘Care’".

Currently distilling into Principles and Practice: Practical Empathy by Indi Young, Sand Talk by Tyson Yunkaporta, Living a Feminist Life by Sara Ahmed and Lean UX (Designing Great Products with Agile Teams) by Gothelf/Seiden.

Openly learning and unlearning on medusae.club.
